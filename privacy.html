Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is handled when you use the Critism's Voucher Discord Bot and our associated website. By adding the bot to your server or logging into our website, you agree to this policy.

1. Data We Collect

We process or temporarily access data through the following interactions:

A. Via the Discord Bot

  • Identifiers: User IDs, Server IDs (Guild IDs), and Role IDs.
  • Profile Data: Discord usernames and custom server nicknames.
  • Message Content: Text strings processed only when our explicit application commands or prefix systems are triggered.

B. Via Website Authentication (Login with Discord)

  • When you use our website login feature, we use Discord's OAuth2 API to read your public profile information (specifically your User ID, username, and profile avatar link).

2. How We Use Your Data

We strictly utilize the processed data for the following essential structural purposes:

  • To execute, format, and deliver operational voucher commands within your text channels.
  • To retain internal custom server settings chosen by administrators.
  • To properly authenticate your identity and display your profile picture on the web app interface layout.

3. Data Retention & Local Storage

  • Bot Infrastructure: No conversation histories or chat text logs are permanently archived or stored on our servers. Server configurations are kept indefinitely until the bot is removed.
  • Website Infrastructure: Because our application is statically hosted via GitHub Pages, your Discord access token is saved strictly on your local browser memory layer (via localStorage). We do not host a central database that captures or stores your login keys.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease any individual user analytics or identity datasets with third parties. All authentication operations happen directly through Discord's official secure gateway pipelines.

5. Data Removal

If you wish to purge saved voucher settings or server variables, kicking the bot from your server will flag those parameters for deletion. You can remove website profile access at any time by simply clicking the Logout button on the landing dashboard.
