Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your data is handled when you use our Discord Bot. By adding the bot to your server, you agree to this policy.

1. Data We Collect

Our bot may process or temporarily store the following information from Discord:

  • User IDs and Server IDs (Guild IDs)
  • Basic profile data (Username)
  • Message Content (only when commands are triggered)

2. How We Use Data

We only use this data to execute the bot's commands, manage settings for your server, and ensure the proper technical operation of the bot.

3. Data Retention

We do not store your data permanently unless it is strictly required for the bot's functionality (like saving your custom server configuration). No text logs or chat histories are saved permanently.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.
