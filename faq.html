📚 Frequently Asked Questions

⚙️ How do I setup the bot?

Invite the bot and run /setup to configure everything automatically.

❓ Why are slash commands not showing?

Discord may take a few minutes to sync commands. Reinviting the bot can help.

🎫 How do vouchers work?

Users can create and manage vouchers using slash commands in your server.

🟢 Is the bot free?

Yes, all core features are completely free.
